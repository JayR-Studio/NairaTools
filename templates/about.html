About NairaTools

Smart tools for everyday Nigerian money decisions.

NairaTools helps Nigerians understand their money better — from salary survival to data plans, loan costs, and dollar conversion.

What NairaTools Does

We build simple tools that help you check if your salary can last, compare data plans, understand loan interest, and convert dollar to naira.

Why It Exists

Many people earn money but still struggle before month end. NairaTools helps you see where your money is going and make clearer decisions.

Who It Is For

Salary earners, students, small business owners, freelancers, and anyone trying to manage money better in Nigeria.

Important Note

NairaTools provides estimates and practical guidance. It does not replace professional financial advice. Always verify important money decisions.
